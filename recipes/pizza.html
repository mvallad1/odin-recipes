Pizza

This is a recipe to make pizza at home. Please make sure you have all necessary ingredients before you begin. This recipe will take approximately 2 hours.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Topping of your choice
  • Olive oil
  • Cornmeal or flour

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on your pizza dough package or recipe (usually around 425째F to 475째F or 220째C to 240째C).
  2. Prepare your pizza dough according to the instructions if using homemade dough. If using store-bought dough, remove it from the packaging and let it sit at room temperature for about 20-30 minutes to allow it to relax.
  3. Lightly dust a clean surface with flour and stretch or roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape (round, square, etc.). If you prefer a thicker crust, leave it a bit thicker; for a thinner crust, roll it out more thinly.
  4. Place the stretched dough onto a pizza peel or a parchment-lined baking sheet lightly dusted with cornmeal or flour to prevent sticking.
  5. Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, covering the entire pizza.
  7. Add your desired toppings over the cheese. Be mindful not to overload the pizza, as it may become too heavy and soggy during baking.
  8. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza for added flavor and to help brown the crust.
  9. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Once done, carefully remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  11. Enjoy!
